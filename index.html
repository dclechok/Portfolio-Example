·About Me·

I've been passionate about web development since a young kid growing up in the 90's. After tinkering around in the early years of HTML, on various free web hosting services, I decided that I wanted to invest in my very own domain name. My parents weren't "believers" in the internet, so I had a better chance of them giving me their paychecks than actually paying for one...

The only option was to set out on my own... I found work for someone who was a domain name trader, and she offered to give me a free domain name if I somehow pushed traffic to a website of hers. Somehow, I figured out just how to do that, and I finally received my first domain name.

That was many years ago. After some college, workshops, and countless hours tinkering around in different programming languages, as a hobbyist, I'm finally back to my roots, and where it all started -- Web Development.
